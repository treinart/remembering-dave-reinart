# Remembering Dave Reinart

**David Allen "Dave" Reinart**  
April 27, 1954 - May 14, 2026  
Tomah, Wisconsin

Live memorial page: <https://remembering-dave-reinart.netlify.app/>

This site was made for Dad.

It is a place for family, friends, and the people who knew Dave to find the Celebration of Life details, read his obituary, see photos, share the page, and remember the stories he left behind.

Dave built a life in Tomah through family, work, friendships, stubborn opinions, good stories, time outdoors, laughter, and the kind of conversations that stayed with people. This page is here so those memories have a place to live.

## Goodbye Dad

Dad,

We made this page with love so the people who knew you can gather around the stories, laughter, and memories you left with all of us.

Thank you for the family you built, the work you did, the stories you told, and the love you gave in your own way. Thank you for teaching us where we came from, for keeping family stories alive, and for giving us memories we will keep carrying forward.
